About the Workshop

We meet once a month to tackle rich problems, swap strategies, and build mathematical habits of mind. Sessions are split by level, so newcomers and competition veterans both find a challenge.

Hands-on Problems

Work in small groups on geometry, number theory, algebra, combinatorics, and logic.

Proofs, not tricks

Go beyond answers: learn how and why solutions work. Induction, invariants, parity & more.

Friendly Prep

Supportive practice for AMO/IMO style contests — no pressure, lots of curiosity.

FAQ

Who can attend?

Years 7–13, split into beginner/intermediate/advanced tracks. Parents and teachers welcome to observe.

What do I bring?

Pencils, ruler, curiosity. If online, a camera-on setup helps for collaboration.

Does it cost anything?

The workshop is community-run and currently free. We may suggest a small donation to cover room hire.
